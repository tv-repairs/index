Meet Tom Vance

The "TV" in TV Repairs

Hello! I'm Tom Vance. Throughout my life, I've found that navigating health and wellness can often feel like trying to watch a television with a bad antenna—there's too much static, conflicting advice, and noise.

Tuning Into Your Clear Channel

That's where the idea of "TV Repairs" comes from. My initials are TV, but the philosophy goes deeper. I believe that true health isn't about rigid diets or exhausting exercise routines. It's about slowly adjusting the dial until things come through clearly.

We all have our own unique "clear channel"—a state where our bodies and minds feel aligned, energetic, and at peace. My goal as a coach is to help you remove the interference and tune into that frequency.

The Turtle Philosophy

You might notice the turtle theme across my site. To me, the turtle represents the ultimate approach to sustainable health: slow, steady, and resilient. Quick fixes and crash courses might give you a burst of progress, but they often lead to burnout. By taking the steady path, we build habits that last a lifetime.
